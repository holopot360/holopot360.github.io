About us

Welcome to Inventor's Kitchen, where new ideas are cooking. We are a family of scientists and engineers driven by a shared passion for innovation. Our name was inspired by the fact that most of our work is done in our free time at home—often in the kitchen—where everyday life and creativity intersect.


Our work is a labor of love—an effort to explore novel ways of creating immersive experiences. We continue to refine our prototypes and are always excited to discuss ideas and potential collaborations with anyone who shares our interest in technology and creativity.


Whether you’re a fellow enthusiast, researcher, or simply curious about our work, we invite you to reach out. We value discussion, collaboration, and the exchange of ideas as we continue to improve our prototypes, experiment with new concepts and discuss new ideas.


Please feel free to contact us via email or connect on LinkedIn. We look forward to engaging with others who are as passionate about innovation as we are.
